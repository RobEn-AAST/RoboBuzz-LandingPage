About

RoboBuzz is a dynamic robotics competition organized by RobEn club, designed to challenge participants in software, hardware, embedded systems, and electrical engineering knowledge. The competition features structured rounds, real-time scoring, and diverse question formats ranging from multiple choice to hands-on challenges.

Behind the scenes, dedicated teams handle everything from question development and scoring rules to competition software and event logistics â€” ensuring an engaging and professional experience for all competitors.

Eligibility

  • Open to all students from AASTMT
  • Teams of 4 members
  • At least one member with coding skills
  • At least one member familiar with hardware or electrical
  • At least one member familiar with mechanical engineering
  • All team members must be present on the competition day
