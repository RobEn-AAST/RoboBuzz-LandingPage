About

RoboBuzz is a dynamic robotics competition organized by RobEn club, designed to challenge participants in software, hardware, embedded systems, and electrical engineering knowledge. The competition features structured rounds, real-time scoring, and diverse question formats ranging from multiple choice to hands-on challenges.

Our team works hard to create fun and fair challenges, clear scoring, and smooth event organization. We want every RoboBuzz competitor to have an exciting and rewarding experience.

Eligibility

Electrical
Control systems & circuits
  • Open to all enrolled students of AASTMT
  • Teams must consist of exactly four members
  • At least one team member must be familiar with software programming
  • At least one team member must be familiar with mechanical engineering/hardware
  • At least one team member must be familiar with electrical engineering/control systems
  • All team members are required to attend the competition in person
