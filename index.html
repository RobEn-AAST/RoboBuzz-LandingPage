About

RoboBuzz is a dynamic robotics competition organized by RobEn club, designed to challenge participants in software, hardware, embedded systems, mechanical, and electrical engineering knowledge. The competition features structured rounds, real-time scoring, and diverse question formats ranging from multiple choice to hands-on challenges.

Our team works hard to create fun and fair challenges, clear scoring, and smooth event organization. We want every RoboBuzz competitor to have an exciting and rewarding experience.
